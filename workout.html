The Welcome Back Partner Circuit

A scalable, shared-rep park workout for two. Tap any card to mark it as complete as you move through the circuit.

40-45 Mins 2 People

How It Works

  • • The Run: Both partners run the 200m distance together at an easy pace.
  • • The Stations (YGIG): "You Go, I Go." One works while the other rests. Break up the reps however you want (e.g., switch every 5-10 reps).

Run 1

200 Meters

Station 1

Banded Ski Pulls

60 Reps Total (30 each)

Bands looped high on the crossbar. Hinge at the hips and pull hands down and back.

Run 2

200 Meters

Station 2

TRX Squats

60 Reps Total (30 each)

Use the TRX for balance to sit deep, pulling up slightly if the legs get heavy.

Run 3

200 Meters

Station 3

Pushups

40-60 Reps Total (20-30 each)

If toes on grass gets tough, use the TRX straps or a park bench for incline pushups.

Run 4

200 Meters

Station 4

KB Farmer's Carry

100 Meters Total (50m each)

Pick a 25m turnaround point. One walks, one rests, then swap.

Run 5

200 Meters

Station 5

TRX Rows

60 Reps Total (30 each)

Lean back and row chest to handles. Step back to make it easier, closer to make it harder.

Run 6

200 Meters

Station 6

Walking Lunges

60 Steps Total (30 each)

Keep the chest up and lightly tap the back knee to the grass. Swap every 10 steps.

Run 7

200 Meters

Station 7

Dumbbell Push Press

60 Reps Total (30 each)

Light dip in the knees to drive the dumbbells overhead. Use the legs to help the arms.

Run 8

200 Meters (Final Sprint!)

Done!

Grab some water, stretch out, and celebrate.
